# Project PRD: Gemshouse Luxury Gemstone Marketplace & Management Suite

## 1. Executive Summary

**Gemshouse** is a world-class luxury gemstone and diamond marketplace (gemshouse.shop) specializing in natural gemstones, fancy diamonds, and wholesale sourcing. The platform serves as both a high-fidelity B2C/B2B storefront and a robust enterprise-grade business management suite for global operations.

## 2. Target Audiences

- **B2B**: Gemstone wholesalers, jewelry manufacturers, retailers, and diamond traders.
- **B2C**: High-net-worth gemstone collectors and luxury consumers.
- **Internal**: Gemshouse inventory managers, sales executives, and executive leadership.

## 3. Brand Identity & Design System: "Lustre & Legacy"

The visual language is designed to evoke the prestige of Cartier, Rolex, and Sotheby's.

### Visual Foundations

- **Primary Palette**: Deep Emerald Green (#0F5132), Champagne Gold (#D4AF37), Charcoal Black (#1A1A1A).
- **Surface**: Pure Linen White (#FDFDFD) for an editorial, high-whitespace aesthetic.
- **Typography**:
  - _Headings_: Playfair Display (Elegant Luxury Serif).
  - _Body/UI_: Montserrat (Modern Sans-Serif).
- **Core Elements**: Glassmorphism (subtle), thin champagne-gold dividers, high-contrast macro photography.

## 4. Platform Modules & Key Features

### A. Customer-Facing Marketplace (Desktop & Mobile)

- **Immersive Homepage**: Cinematic hero sections with WebGL sparkle shaders.
- **Advanced Collection Grid**: Deep filtering (Gem type, Carat, Origin, Certification) with infinite scroll.
- **Precision Product Details**: 360-degree viewers, macro-zoom galleries, and dynamic "Dealer Pricing" gateways.
- **B2B Wholesale Hub**: Dedicated portal for bulk sourcing, tiered pricing, and private vault access.
- **Concierge Services**: Booking systems for private consultations and custom sourcing requests.

### B. Business Management Suite (Aesthetica Enterprise)

- **Executive Dashboard**: Q3 Performance monitoring, revenue trends, and real-time portfolio status.
- **Inventory Engine**: High-density stone management with bulk import/export and GIA/GRS compliance tracking.
- **Luxury CRM**: Kanban-based lead pipeline with timeline activities and WhatsApp/Email integration.
- **Inquiry Management**: Centralized hub for product inquiries and bespoke sourcing requests.
- **Certification Compliance**: Automated expiry alerts and lab report verification for global grading standards.
- **Global Logistics**: International order fulfillment, export documentation (Packing Lists, Invoices), and shipment tracking.

## 5. Technical Requirements

- **Responsive Architecture**: Unified master navigation across all breakpoints.
- **Performance**: High-speed image loading for macro-photography.
- **Security**: Role-based access control (RBAC) with comprehensive audit trails for team management.
- **Integrations**: Global mapping (Atelier locations) and WhatsApp Business API.

## 6. Success Metrics

- **Inventory Turnover**: Growth in dealer account acquisition.
- **Inquiry Quality**: Conversion rate from inquiry to qualified negotiation.
- **Operational Efficiency**: Reduction in manual documentation time for international exports.
